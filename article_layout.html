Finding your way around Canvas

Dashboard

The dashboard is the first thing you see when you log in to Canvas and shows the global navigation bar, your courses, your to do list and any recent feedback you have received. The dashboard will also display global announcements to alert you of any important updates. You can customise your display by selecting card view, list view or recent activity mode through the options menu on the top right. Learn more about the general dashboard (Canvas Guides) and the dashboard sidebar (Canvas Guides) through the Canvas guides.

Welcome to Canvas

Canvas offers all your study needs in one space, including course content, lecture recordings, online assessment submissions points as well as marks and feedback.

Accessing Canvas

You can access Canvas by through the URL https://lms.griffith.edu.au/ or by simply clicking the link on the top right of your myGriffith site.

You will lose access to the Canvas system if you:

  • Have graduated from your program
  • Have deferred your study
  • Have had your enrolment suspended for any reason
  • Are a HDR student and have submitted your final thesis

If one of your enrolled courses is not coming up in Canvas, there could be a number of reasons as to why.
